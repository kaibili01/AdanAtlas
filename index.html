PRTHPRO — Digital Tools for Tribal Housing Equity

This responsive website was created to support the Pit River Tribe’s Housing Department and Housing Board by providing a centralized, accessible platform that outlines their responsibilities, services, and governance processes. By making key information transparent and available across devices, the platform promotes accountability and builds trust within the tribal community. It also serves as a resource for education, economic empowerment, and pathways to homeownership for tribal members.

ESSA Tribal Consultation Plan

A culturally responsive framework designed to ensure meaningful consultation between Tribal Nations and state education agencies under ESSA. The project promotes transparent, reciprocal partnerships that support Indigenous student success through cyclical evaluation and accountability mechanisms.

farmShare — Connecting Communities Through Urban Harvests

By leveraging technology, farmShare strategically directs resources toward communities facing poverty, food insecurity, limited healthcare access, and educational challenges. The project aimed to produce a handheld app that connects urban fruit tree owners with neighbors in need. This targeted intervention promotes equity by maximizing impact and improving overall community well-being.

Grupo Huellas: Trail Traces Group

A cross-border environmental campaign created to address the ecological threat of discarded automobile tires polluting the Tijuana Estuary — a vital wildlife refuge shared by the U.S. and Mexico. This project brought together NOAA, the Tijuana Estuary Reserve, CalRecycle, Earth Island Institute, the Goldman School of Public Policy, and local Baja California government to launch a binational initiative raising awareness and catalyzing action for the estuary's preservation.

Facebook Orange — Workplace Wellness

A wellness innovation concept blending digital reach with real human connection. We proposed tools such as an internal platform to build micro-communities across departments, and a real-time projection monitor where employees could share thoughts and emotional reflections about ongoing work—without judgment.

The platform helped remote teams discover shared interests, like gaming, and organize real-world meetups during company-wide events, bridging physical distance with meaningful interaction. By using tech to encourage in-person engagement, the project redefined how corporate wellness can foster community and belonging.
